About This Programme

This is not a tutorial app. Khan Chacha is a real client with a real problem — their maintenance request process is broken. We are building the system that fixes it.

Students learn vibe coding: describing outcomes to an AI pair-programmer (Claude Code), reviewing the generated code, and shipping features they could not have written alone. This is how professionals build today.
